This toolbox generates the modulation spectrum of sounds based on the procedures described by the following paper.

Nai Ding, Aniruddh D. Patel, Lin Chen, Henry Butler, Cheng Luo, David Poeppel,
Temporal modulations in speech and music,
Neuroscience & Biobehavioral Reviews,
Volume 81, Part B,
2017,
